DesKilo is a free, privacy-first app for small self-organized coworking communities.

• Draw your workspace on a grid: levels, offices, desks and bookable seats
• Tap a seat on the live floor plan to check in — walk-ups are conflict-safe
• Reserve ahead, once or as a recurring series, with a time scroller to browse any moment
• Calendar of your bookings; admins see everyone's
• Fair money: membership plans with half-day quotas, one transparent ledger per member
• Community expenses: buy coffee for the space, get it credited on your next statement
• Everything an admin does for you needs your confirmation

No Google services, no tracking, no ads. Self-hostable backend. MIT-licensed.
